Two mechanical traps if you extend these tests: SPEC's blockquote front matter is a changelog *about* the corrections, so a naive "the string `apply_patch` must not appear" scan trips on it — scan the body after the first `---`; and `test_the_annotated_config_example_parses_and_is_exactly_the_defaults` asserts `Settings.model_validate(example) == Settings()`, which is what makes the copy-paste block provably a list of real defaults rather than a wish.
